Connect with Shalom

Sunday Programme

Each Sunday we gather as two congregations (English and Mandarin), in smaller classes to learn the Bible in a more intimate setting; we also meet in the main sanctuary for the corporate worship service.

The English-speaking congregation meets at 9:30am for smaller classes and at 11:00am for worship service.

Worship Service

The worship service begins at 11:00am and lasts about 1-1/2 hours. There is a time of singing, corporate praying and preaching, when the Bible is taught and applied by the speaker. We observe the Lord's Supper every first Sunday of the month. On months with a fifth Sunday, we have a combined service with the Mandarin congregation that is bilingual (English and Mandarin).

Bible-Study Classes

These classes start at 9:30 and last for one hour. These are the various groups:

  • Senior adults: Agape Room
  • Young adults: Room
  • Youth (multiple groups): Room
  • Primary-school-level Sunday School: Room
  • Pre-school Sunday School: Room

Mandarin Congregation

The Mandarin-speaking congregation meets at 9am for the worship service, and then holds smaller classes and special meetings from 11:00am till about 12:30pm.

Week-day Small Groups

During the week we meet in small community groups at members' homes for mutual encouragement, prayer and process life issues with the Bible.

Ladies Group
  • Wednesday evenings
  • Taman Jurong
  • Magdalene Chuah

Shalom ladies meet regularly to share our lives with one another, to support one another spiritually, emotionally and physically.

Senior Men's Group
  • Tuesday evenings
  • Taman Jurong
  • Chuah Sze Seng

We meet every alternate week to hear from other men, and learn how to do our work, love our family and serve the church as God's Men.

Young Adults Group
  • Friday evenings
  • Various Locations
  • Victor Toh

The young adults are a group in transition from school to the working world. We meet to support one another and learn how the gospel speaks to our new vocational, spiritual and social dimensions of life.

Senior Men's Group
  • Friday evenings
  • Gek Poh
  • Ps Anthony Yeoh

The Gek Poh small group is a cosy fellowship of older married couples. We love local fare and often meet over food to discuss how the Bible relates to our different life stages.
