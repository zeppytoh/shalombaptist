What is the essential message of Christianity?

In the beginning God created the heavens and the earth. He made everything good, and the apex of creation is mankind. You and I have been made in the image of God.

We were created to glorify God and enjoy him forever

The Bible declares that we were placed here, not by chance as secular humanism tells us, but by a loving God who made us to know and enjoy his goodness, and to love him back.

But we have been separated from his perfect holiness because collectively, the human race chose not to obey and worship him as God, but to serve and pursue our own ends. As a result, a gulf between us exists, and the world is no longer as it should be, because we are not living as we should live. We could be living in loving communion with our Creator God, who reveals himself as Our Father.
